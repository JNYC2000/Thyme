Lobster Roll

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked lobster meat
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 split-top brioche buns
  • Chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Chop lobster meat into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix with mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush buns with melted butter and toast lightly.
  4. Fill buns with lobster mixture.
  5. Garnish with chives and enjoy.
